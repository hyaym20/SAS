Who We Are?

We are a company that provides a computerized maintenance management system (CMMS) specialized in managing medical devices by saving all medical device data, including all the services needed from installation to condemnation across all the medical companies and hospitals in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Our Vision

To be the first leading platform for managing and tracking medical devices maintenance in the Kingdom in order to improve patient experience and contribute to the digital transformation, which effectively aims to achieve Vision 2030 in the health sector.
