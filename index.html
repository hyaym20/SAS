Who We Are?

Saudi Asset Solutions (SAS) is a forward-thinking technology company dedicated to revolutionizing asset management within the healthcare sector. Our flagship product, SAS+, is an advanced Computerized Maintenance Management System (CMMS) designed to optimize medical device management in hospitals. By streamlining workflows, reducing equipment downtime, and enhancing maintenance efficiency, SAS+ empowers biomedical engineers to deliver optimal device performance and prioritize patient safety across healthcare facilities in Saudi Arabia.

About SAS+

SAS+ is a comprehensive Computerized Maintenance Management System (CMMS) tailored specifically for managing medical devices in hospitals. It offers a robust suite of features designed to optimize hospital operations, including work order management, inventory control, preventive maintenance scheduling, and regulatory compliance tracking. By providing real-time insights into device performance and maintenance history, SAS+ enables biomedical engineers to make data-driven decisions, optimize resource allocation, and ultimately ensure a higher standard of patient care.
